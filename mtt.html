Team FourStare

Team FourStare is one of Year Up - Chicago's Class 12, Mod III teams composed of Jesus Garcia, Leslie Hodges, Kyle Little and Estefany Salcedo.

This team is full of proactive, innovative and genuine minds who, coincidently, all wear glasses. The goal of this web site was to show everyone's skills in a collaborative fashion. This website is a product of the hard work Team FourStare has put in since the day it was introduced. Please enjoy our web page and feel free to contact us at team.fourstare@gmail.com if you have any comments or feedback. We are always looking to improve and we encourage you to let us know if you would like to see any changes!
